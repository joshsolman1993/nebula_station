# Audio Assets Required

Please download the following sound files and place them in this directory (`client/public/sounds/`).

## Sound Effects (SFX)
- `ui_click.mp3`: A subtle sci-fi click or beep. Used for navigation and buttons.
- `success.mp3`: A positive chime. Used for completing quests or successful actions.
- `error.mp3`: A negative buzzer or low beep. Used for errors.
- `build.mp3`: Mechanical construction sound. Used when building structures.
- `combat.mp3`: Laser fire or explosion. Used for battle reports.
- `coins.mp3`: Digital coin sound or credit transfer sound. Used for market transactions.

## Background Music (BGM)
- `bgm.mp3`: Ambient space music. Looping track.

## Recommended Sources
- [Kenney Assets (Sci-Fi Sounds)](https://kenney.nl/assets/sci-fi-sounds)
- [Freesound.org](https://freesound.org/)
